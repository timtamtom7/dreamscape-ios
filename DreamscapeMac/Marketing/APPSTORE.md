## Description

DreamscapeMac transforms your nightly visions into a personal journey of discovery. Record your dreams with rich detail, uncover recurring symbols, and gain AI-powered insights into what your subconscious mind is telling you.

### Features

- **📝 Dream Journal** — Capture dreams with timestamps, mood, and vivid details
- **🔮 Symbol Recognition** — Identify recurring dream symbols and their personal significance
- **🤖 AI Analysis** — Get thoughtful interpretations powered by local AI
- **📊 Sleep Correlations** — Discover patterns between your dreams and sleep quality
- **✨ Cosmic Interface** — A beautifully dark, celestial aesthetic that feels like exploring the night sky

### Why DreamscapeMac?

- All data stays on your Mac — your dreams are private
- Beautiful dark mode designed for nighttime use
- Symbol library with 100+ common dream archetypes
- Community dream sharing (opt-in, anonymous)
